User Guide for the Ball Box

Welcome to the Draw Ball Box!
This app is designed to create various shapes and forms by manipulating balls. You can add, move, and interact with the balls to form interesting patterns and designs.

1. Adding a New Ball:

Click on the beachball icon to add a new ball to the box. The ball will be randomly colored and will appear in the box.

2. Selecting a Ball:

Click on the ball to select it. The selected ball will get bright red color for the time of selection. To unselect the ball, click on it again.

3. Moving the Ball:

Once a ball is selected, use the arrow keys (up, down, left, right) on the keyboard to move it in the desired direction. The ball will avoid collisions with other balls and will bounce away if a collision occurs!

4. Removing a Ball:

To remove the selected ball, press the Backspace key on the keyboard.

5. Moving the Ball with the Mouse:

Double-click anywhere inside the container to move the selected ball to that location. The ball will be placed at the point where you clicked.When positions of two balls collide inside the container, the affected ball will trigger a shake effect.

6. Interacting with Balls:

You can only have one selected ball at a time. Whenever you click on a ball, any previously selected balls will be unselected.

This app allows you to create and interact with a variety of shapes and designs formed by balls. Have fun experimenting with different patterns and arrangements!
